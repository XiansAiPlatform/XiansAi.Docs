## What You Get

<div style="display: grid; grid-template-columns: repeat(auto-fit, minmax(300px, 1fr)); gap: 1.5rem; margin: 2rem 0;">
  <div style="border: 1px solid var(--xians-border-medium); border-radius: 0.5rem; padding: 1.5rem;">
    <h3 style="margin-top: 0;">Multi-Tenancy</h3>
    <p>Complete isolation of agents, workflows, data, and conversations. Mix system-scoped and tenant-scoped deployments as needed.</p>
  </div>

  <div style="border: 1px solid var(--xians-border-medium); border-radius: 0.5rem; padding: 1.5rem;">
    <h3 style="margin-top: 0;">Event-Driven Execution</h3>
    <p>Trigger agents with user messages, webhooks, cron schedules, or timers. Built-in routing and retries.</p>
  </div>

  <div style="border: 1px solid var(--xians-border-medium); border-radius: 0.5rem; padding: 1.5rem;">
    <h3 style="margin-top: 0;">Long-Running Workflows</h3>
    <p>Built on Temporal for workflows that run for days, months, or longer with automatic retry, recovery, and resumption.</p>
  </div>

  <div style="border: 1px solid var(--xians-border-medium); border-radius: 0.5rem; padding: 1.5rem;">
    <h3 style="margin-top: 0;">Distributed Execution</h3>
    <p>Stateless activities with automatic load balancing. Scale by adding workers. Fault-tolerant by design.</p>
  </div>

  <div style="border: 1px solid var(--xians-border-medium); border-radius: 0.5rem; padding: 1.5rem;">
    <h3 style="margin-top: 0;">Human-in-the-Loop</h3>
    <p>Workflows pause for human inputâ€”hours, days, or weeksâ€”then automatically resume. Task creation, draft approvals, and audit trails.</p>
  </div>

  <div style="border: 1px solid var(--xians-border-medium); border-radius: 0.5rem; padding: 1.5rem;">
    <h3 style="margin-top: 0;">Knowledge Store</h3>
    <p>Versioned repository for prompts and domain knowledge. Agents can read, write, and update their own prompts over time.</p>
  </div>

  <div style="border: 1px solid var(--xians-border-medium); border-radius: 0.5rem; padding: 1.5rem;">
    <h3 style="margin-top: 0;">Communication</h3>
    <p>Secure async messaging with WebSocket, SSE, or REST. API keys or OIDC auth. Multi-conversation and topic-based organization.</p>
  </div>

  <div style="border: 1px solid var(--xians-border-medium); border-radius: 0.5rem; padding: 1.5rem;">
